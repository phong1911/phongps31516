Sauce
Nguyên liệu Khối lượng
Seaweed 15 gr
Water 2 liter
Katsuobushi 20 gr
Japanese soy sauce 100 ml
Mirin 50 ml
Egg 2 pcs
Whipping 20 ml
Sauce 10 gr
Parmesan 15 gr
Milk 20 ml

Making:
Add seaweed, Katsuobushi, soy sauce, mirin, and water to cook together for 2 hours.
Use 2 egg yolks mixed with the cooked sauce, whipping, parmesan and milk.
Low heat stir with noodles until thickened.
