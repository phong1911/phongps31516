DOUGH
Nguyên liệu Khối lượng
Flour (13% protein) 1 kg
Slat 20 gr
Fresh yeast 3 gr
Water 650 ml

Making:
Sift the flour with the correct amount to beat.
Measure the mixture of water, salt yeast. Stir to dissolve. Then add the flour in the flour mill.
Beat 18 minutes. Then make pizza ball weight 210g/ball.
Incubate in the dough box to size 13cm. Put it in the fridge after checking the quality.
